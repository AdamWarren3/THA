A personal identity framework named by analogy to the real Adam optimizer lineage. Adam (2014) and AdamW (2017) are both real, published optimizers — each refines the update rule, reducing overfitting and improving generalization. AdamW³ extends that lineage by name only, not in the literature: the third iteration, applied to the self. Not who I was, not who I became, but the optimized version that emerged from repeated correction. The mechanism — recursive self-correction, the update rule turned on its own output — is general and available to anyone; the name is autobiographical, and only the name. Technical, spiritual, and philosophical dimensions unified in a single identifier. The optimizer became the optimized.
